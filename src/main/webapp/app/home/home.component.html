Library

Library allows you to find out more about the music you love through Spotify's Song Features. We import your playlists into our database and give you information about your tracks in an easy-to-digest manner.

Take advantage of the advanced filters to query our database in the way YOU want. You can filter by Track Features, Populartiy and even by Producers. After filtering the songs you want, you can select them to add them to the Staging Playlist. From the staging playlist, you can export your curated playlist onto Spotify.

How It Works


ReCAPD allows you to find the musicians and
artists behind the music you love.

We scan your Spotify library, and use our
proprietary dataset to find the tracks you love
on the MusicBrainz database.


We then scan this data to find the producers,
guitarists, bassists (and more) that are credited
in the most songs in your library.


Simply select the type of artist you want to
find, select the timeframe, and from where
you want to scan, and we do the rest!


Your favourite musician isn't in the list?
Consider contributing to MusicBrainz and
get them recognised!


Click here to learn more about our data
methodology and download our dataset.

We pull data using the Spotify API to determine your top artists and fetch your playlists. The visualization can be tailored to reflect different time periods, and connections can be toggled based on artist similarity.

Network offers a deep dive into your musical preferences, providing insights into the artists and genres you love the most.

Visualisations

Visualisations illustrates your listening habits by extracting data directly from your Spotify account. We pull data directly from the Spotify API to determine the top genres and artists from your selected playlist. Visualisations can also provide more detailed insights into your favorite playlists. It provides an average value for:

Danceability: This is a value that describes how suitable a track is for dancing based on musical elements such as tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity.

Energy: This is a value that represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Typically, energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy.

Tempo: average tempo of the playlist in beats per minute

Acousticness: confidence measure from 0 to 1 of whether the track is acoustic



Vault

Vault is uniquely designed to secure your favorite music playlists. Whether it's your Spotify-generated Discover Weekly, Release Radar, or any Daily Mixes, Vault ensures you keep a backup of the playlists you love.

Set Vault to save your playlists daily, weekly, or monthly. Customize the frequency to fit your listening habits.

While Vault is perfect for automatically generated playlists, it’s also fully capable of handling any playlist you cherish.

Set it once and forget it. Vault takes care of the rest, ensuring your playlists are saved and accessible anytime, anywhere.
