  • Use or view our website via your browser's cookies.

How will we use your data?

Lowertones collects your data so that we can:

  • Provide you with our services, manage your account.
  • Email you with special offers on other products and services we think you might like.

How do we store your data?

Lowertones securely stores your data, which is encrypted in our backend.

Lowertones will keep your User Data and Third Party Data until you have not used Lowertones or any of our services for 2 years. You may also request the termination of your account and the deletion of your data. Once this time period has passed without any usage, or you have requested account deletion, we will delete your data within 1 month.


Marketing

Lowertones would like to send you information about products and services of ours that we think you might like. If you have agreed to receive marketing, you may always opt out at a later date. You have the right at any time to stop Lowertones from contacting you for marketing purposes.


What are your data protection rights?

Lowertones would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access - You have the right to request Lowertones for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification - You have the right to request that Lowertones correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Lowertones to complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure - You have the right to request that Lowertones erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that Lowertones restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to Lowertones’ processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability - You have the right to request that Lowertones transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our emails, at the end of our privacy policy.


What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.


How do we use cookies?

Our Company uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:

  • Keeping you signed in
  • Saving your accessibility settings

What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Functionality - Lowertones uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and accessibility settings.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.
